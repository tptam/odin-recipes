Marinated Raisins

Like sun-dried tomatoes, raisins go well with olive oil and chili. Place a spoonful on a slice of baguette, or cheese, or both.

Ingredients

  • Raisins: 100g
  • Olive oil: 100cc
  • Wine vinegar: 2 tbsp
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Dried whole red chili pepper: 1
  • Dried basil: 1/2 tsp
  • Dried oregano: 1/2 tsp

Steps

  1. Soak raisins in warm water for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain and pat dry with paper towel.
  3. Add salt to vinegar and mix well.
  4. Slowly add the olive oil and whisk until the mixture is a little thick.
  5. Add basil and oregano.
  6. Place raisins and chili in a sterilized jar. Pour the mixture so that all the raisins are covered. (Add more olive oil if needed.)
  7. Close the lid and store in a dark, cool place. They are ready to eat after a few days.
